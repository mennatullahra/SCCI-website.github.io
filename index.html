SCCI.

Seek The Peak SCCI is a Student Conference on Communication and Information that is held annually, organized by students for students under the auspices of the Faculty of Computers and Information, Cairo University.

About Us

SCCI is a typical simulation of the outside real life. An A+ student can be very high skilled technically during college time yet the scenario that usually takes place is that he doesn't find a place among others in the outside competitive job market, a market where what distinguishes one graduate from another is the soft skills and the practical life experience. That was the main purpose behind the creation of SCCI since the very beginning, which was bridging the gap between the technical life and the practical life in the market place. SCCI provides its participants with the technical skills and soft skills required in the form of sessions given throughout the year. SCCI will run as a set of workshops concluded by a three days conference. The workshops will span eight to nine weeks period (once a week) to give students trainees (Participants) the chance to digest information and for student instructors to prepare instructional material. SCCi is one eventful experience that will stay in your heart & you'll watch it in your personality development. "Inside each and every one of us, deep inside the core of each one's being is a winner waiting to be awakened and unleashed upon the world" and that is what SCCi is all about, unleashing your potentials.

Mission

Our mission is to decrease the gap between the academic life and market needs through two specific ways: - By applying into one of our 10 committees to be an officially SCCI member. - Or by applying in one of our five unique workshops "ADMIT" that are held every year.
